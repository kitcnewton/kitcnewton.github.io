I am interested in multiscale inverse kinetic theory. My current focus is on approaching multiscale inverse problems from a Bayesian perspective. More specifically, I study the ill-posedness of certain inverse problems in a multiscale context, using Bayesian techniques. My bachelor's degree is in physics.

Here is a list of my publications:

    in preparation.

    arXiv:TBA
    Coming soon.

  2. Classical and Quantum Mechanical Motion in Magnetic Fields
    American Journal of Physics, to appear.
    with Joel Franklin
    arXiv:1603.01211
    We study the motion of a particle in a particular magnetic field configuration both classically and quantum mechanically.

  3. The dynamics of the Schrodinger-Newton system with self-field coupling
    Classical and Quantum Gravity.
    with Joel Franklin, Yudan Guo, and Maximilian Schlosshauer.
    arXiv:1603.03380
    We probe the dynamics of a modified form of the Schrodinger-Newton (SN) system of gravity coupled to single particle quantum mechanics.
