I'm a fourth year grad student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My interests lie in multiscale inverse kinetic theory. My current work is on the applications of Bayesian statistics to various problems involving diffuse optical tomography. My advisor is Qin Li. In Summer 2020 I will be a visitor at MPI-MiS Leipzig working with Felix Otto and Andre Uschmajew. In Fall 2020 I will be an assistant professor in math at Diablo Valley College, in addition to being affiliated with MSRI.
